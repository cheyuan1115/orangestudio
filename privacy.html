概述

Orange Studio（以下簡稱「我們」）重視您的隱私。本政策說明我們的應用程式如何處理您的資料。

我們收集的資料

我們的應用程式不會主動收集任何個人識別資料。

應用程式中包含 Google AdMob 廣告服務，AdMob 可能會根據您的設定收集廣告識別符（IDFA）以提供個人化廣告。您可以在 iOS 設定中選擇限制廣告追蹤。

本地儲存

部分資料（如設定偏好、歷史紀錄）僅儲存在您的裝置本地，不會上傳至任何伺服器。

第三方服務

  • Google AdMob：廣告投放服務，請參閱 Google 隱私權政策

Overview

Orange Studio ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains how our apps handle your data.

Data We Collect

Our apps do not actively collect any personally identifiable information.

Our apps include Google AdMob advertising. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (IDFA) based on your settings to serve personalized ads. You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings.

Local Storage

Some data (such as preferences and history) is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Ad serving service. See Google Privacy Policy
