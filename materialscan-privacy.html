概述

Orange Studio（以下簡稱「我們」）重視您的隱私。本政策說明「材料辨識+」如何處理您的資料。

相片使用說明

當您拍攝或選取照片進行材料辨識時，該照片會傳送至 Google Gemini AI 服務進行分析，以辨識材料種類與回收資訊。照片僅用於單次辨識，不會由 Orange Studio 儲存或用於任何其他用途。

Google Gemini 的資料處理方式請參閱 Google 隱私權政策。

本地儲存

辨識紀錄（含縮圖與結果）僅儲存在您的裝置本地，不會上傳至 Orange Studio 的伺服器。

我們不收集的資料

本 App 不收集任何個人識別資料、位置資料，亦不包含廣告服務。

第三方服務

  • Google Gemini AI：照片分析服務，請參閱 Google 隱私權政策

English

Overview

Orange Studio ("we") respects your privacy. This policy explains how MaterialScan+ handles your data.

Photo Usage

When you take or select a photo for material recognition, that photo is transmitted to Google Gemini AI for analysis to identify material types and recycling information. Photos are used solely for that single recognition request and are not stored or used for any other purpose by Orange Studio.

For Google Gemini's data practices, please see the Google Privacy Policy.

Local Storage

Scan history (including thumbnails and results) is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to Orange Studio's servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

This app does not collect any personally identifiable information or location data, and contains no advertising services.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini AI: Photo analysis service. See Google Privacy Policy
